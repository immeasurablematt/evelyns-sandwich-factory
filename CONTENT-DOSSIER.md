## 2. What makes them different (the story to tell)

These themes repeat across nearly every source and are the core brand differentiators:

1. **Real roasted turkey, every day.** They roast a whole turkey in-house each morning —
   "real turkey, not pressed," "not deli slices." Reviewers repeatedly contrast it with cold cuts.
2. **No cold cuts. In-house roasted meats.** Both beef and turkey are roasted on site.
3. **Build-your-own on a checklist card.** Customers get a card to tick off bread, cheese,
   meats and toppings; staff build it fresh **right in front of you.**
4. **Fresh bread from the bakery across the street.** Frequently cited freshness signal.
5. **Homemade soups daily.** A different soup each day — split pea & ham, butternut squash,
   mushroom, turkey soup all mentioned.
6. **Generous portions, fair prices.** "Massive" roast beef; "cheaper and tastier than Subway."
7. **Busy but worth it.** "A very busy little place — so worth the wait" (~15 min at lunch).
   Tip surfaced repeatedly: **call/fax your order ahead to skip the line.**
8. **Friendly, energetic, long-running.** Owner ~20 years' experience, "super personality";
   staff described as warm, friendly, accommodating.
9. **Parking:** ample parking beside the shop; municipal parking on Division St (~400m) nearby.
